Smart House Layout

Project : Smart House Layout
Role :UX&UI Designer/HCD Designer/Concept Designer
Description :The extensive UX connected device allows the user to have full control of their house anywhere and anytime. SHL allows the user to utilize the house space fully, movable built-in-furniture created by the AI, and collectible and shareable furniture designs.
Target Audience : Smart house users who are looking for full customization and smart product control in their home environment by AI. Time line : SHL project was 4 1/2weeks project, All the deliverables are digital and case study.


Product Pitch

The current house market is continuously raising hight that is no longer affordable for many people in the U.S, and smart house products been becoming part of our lives. Where multiple products are existing separately in the house that is controlled by AI, such as Alexa and Google home. Where some users need to set multiple AI in different spaces in order to control the installed smart house products, SHL wants to avoid having multiple AI in a single house. That way, the user will no longer need to suffer installing and finding their AI and provide affordable houses with built-in AI systems that allow users to utilize small house spaces fully with live customization.

Minimalism

The product is to pursue minimalism and utilize the ful space due to user's need without agonizing floor plan limitation. Including minimalism furniture for the users.

Low price

The SHL product presents the total user cutomize-able floor plans of the house and the furniture that can be designed and installed through digital device that is created by themselves and designers.

Customization

User will have full control and continueously change of their floor plan, built-in furniture, and use of space with no extra cost.

Easy & Simple AI

The SHL is AI friendly and other digiral device to improve quick and straightforward interactions with the users.
